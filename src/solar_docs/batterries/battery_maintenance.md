# Battery Maintenance Guide

Proper maintenance extends battery lifespan.

## Lead Acid Batteries
- Check water level monthly
- Use distilled water only
- Avoid deep discharge

## Lithium Batteries
- No water needed
- Keep firmware updated (if smart BMS)
- Avoid extreme temperatures

## Best Practices
- Charge batteries daily
- Avoid overloading
- Ensure good ventilation

## Warning
Poor maintenance can reduce battery life by over 50%.
