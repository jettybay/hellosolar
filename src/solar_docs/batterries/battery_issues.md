# Common Battery Problems

## Battery Drains Too Fast
- Battery is old
- Excessive load
- Insufficient charging

## Battery Not Charging
- Faulty charge controller
- Loose cable
- Solar panel issue

## Battery Swelling or Heating
- Overcharging
- Internal fault
- Immediate shutdown required

## Agent Instruction
Advise customer to switch to grid and disconnect solar if battery is overheating.
