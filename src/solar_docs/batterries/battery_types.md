# Types of Solar Batteries

## 1. Lead Acid Batteries
- Tubular or Gel
- Affordable
- Requires maintenance

## 2. Lithium-Ion Batteries
- Long lifespan
- Fast charging
- Higher cost

## Comparison
| Type | Lifespan | Maintenance |
|----|----|----|
| Lead Acid | 2–4 years | High |
| Lithium | 8–15 years | Low |
