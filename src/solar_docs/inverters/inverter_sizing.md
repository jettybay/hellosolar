# Inverter Sizing Guide

Proper inverter sizing ensures stable power supply and protects your appliances.

## Step 1: Calculate Total Load
List all appliances and their wattage.

| Appliance | Quantity | Wattage |
|---------|---------|---------|
| TV | 1 | 150W |
| Fan | 3 | 75W |
| Refrigerator | 1 | 400W |

Total Load Example: 775W

## Step 2: Add Safety Margin
Add 25–30% buffer.

775W + 30% ≈ 1000W

## Step 3: Choose Inverter Size
- 1kVA inverter ≈ 800W
- 2kVA inverter ≈ 1600W
- 3.5kVA inverter ≈ 2800W

## Call Center Tip
If a customer complains about frequent shutdowns, inverter may be undersized.
