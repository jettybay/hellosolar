# Inverter Basics

An inverter is the brain of a solar power system. It converts DC power from solar panels or batteries into AC power used by household appliances.

## Functions
- Converts DC to AC
- Manages battery charging
- Switches between solar, battery, and grid
- Protects appliances from voltage issues

## Common Brands
- Luminous
- Growatt
- Victron
- SMA
- SolarEdge

## Key Terms
- VA / kVA rating
- Pure sine wave
- Hybrid inverter
- MPPT controller
